Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

NEXUS is open 24/7, 365 days a year. Our front desk is staffed from 5 AM to 10 PM daily for assistance.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?

No! All our memberships are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime with 14 days notice. Annual plans save 15% but require a 12-month commitment.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical or travel reasons. A small administrative fee applies.

Are there lockers and showers?

Absolutely. We provide day-use and permanent lockers, premium showers with toiletries, towel service, and secure changing areas.

Do I need to book classes in advance?

We recommend booking 24 hours ahead for popular classes. Basic members get 2 class credits/month, Pro and Elite have unlimited access.

Is there a minimum age requirement?

Members must be at least 16 years old. Those under 18 require parental consent and must complete a youth orientation.
